# counter

foam run as an actor model тАФ the third resolved pattern, after Locksmith (locks/keys/conditions) and Mechanic (events/tasks/actions). Counter deals in **actors / acts / actions**, and is distinct in being *loop-predicated*: the others experience a loop as an antipattern; Counter's base object *is* a loop тАФ an actor supplying the self with self.

axiom-free, like the core. This package requires `foam` and **not** Mathlib: no choice, no propext. Every claim seals `does not depend on any axioms`. Counter is the application of the physics, so it keeps the physics' discipline. The why is the Lean.

the product sentence, each noun bound to its receipt: **Counter and its user, as a pair, locate the user's relief, restedness, and luck.** Relief is an *address* тАФ a stuck position plus the one deposit that relieves it (`relief_at_position`, core; `illegible_report_is_discovery`). Restedness is a *move* тАФ the homing act, always available, always the user's own (`always_homeable`). Luck is a *timing* тАФ the cross term, met by phase-choice, never farmed (`luck_is_timing`). Located, never instructed; and the pair is load-bearing from both sides тАФ the tool alone has no source (transcription only, the field originates nothing) and the user alone has no view (`observable_relativity_needs_a_third`). These three words are the oldest snake oil in the market, unfalsifiable exactly as long as they name semantics; here they name structure тАФ an address, a move, a timing тАФ and structure carries receipts. That inversion is the market fit.

## the spine so far

`Actor.lean` тАФ the single actor, and the ground facts about settling. An actor's history is a `List G` of acts over its seat's group; the winding number around an act is `Ledger.freq` of that act in the history (Counter *counts*: the name is winding numbers around acts).

- **`replay_is_netAct`** тАФ a whole history collapses to one net displacement, `netAct h : G`. Your entire past is a single group element.
- **`settles_iff_home`** тАФ `Settles S h p тЖФ netAct h = 1`. A history brings you home *iff its net displacement is the identity*. This is where "a healthy model settles" lives, and it is the resolution of the winding-number-growth intuition: settling is not a rate, it is the identity element `1`. Magnitude-free, time-free тАФ the invariant inherits those from the algebra, not from a policy.
- **`always_homeable`** тАФ from *any* accumulated history, appending the single act `sub p (replay h p)` returns you home, unilaterally. "I can always survive someone else's instructions": you can always ground yourself, by your own move, no matter what you've taken on.
- **`lone_actor_settled`** / **`pressure_needs_a_second`** тАФ health is *relational*. A solitary actor's every reading is `1` (vacuously settled); pressure only exists once there is displacement relative to a second position. Priorities.md layer 1 ("your own health") is first but never isolated тАФ even it is defined "in listening to yourself," a relation. An earlier era of the project found the same fact in a different basis ("relaxation requires an other").

`Interleave.lean` тАФ two actors' act-streams interleave into one shared ledger, and the count is on the ledger.

- **`winding_is_own`** тАФ an actor's winding read off the shared ledger equals its winding on its own stream: the interleaving, and the other actor entirely, are invisible to it.
- **`observable_relativity_needs_a_third`** тАФ yet the interleaving is *real*. Two orderings of the same two streams (A-then-B, B-then-A) leave *both* actors' windings identical while differing in the ledger. The relative order of who-acted-when is invisible to each actor and real only in the shared ledger; observing relative motion needs a third bubble that reads the ledger itself. So Counter's minimal *observable* foam is **three, not two** тАФ lining up with dimension-caps-at-three and the three planes.

`Bubble.lean` тАФ the membrane between two actors, in its readable half. The type-level half тАФ cross-winding is a pairing that does not typecheck (`Ledger.freq` demands the act and the history share a type) тАФ cannot be *stated* in Lean, which is the point; these are its shadows on the readable side.

- **`wound_act_is_own`** тАФ any act with nonzero winding in an actor's view is one of the actor's *own* acts. Every circle you can draw is inked around your own acts: you can never draw one around another actor.
- **`other_view_unmoved`** / **`settles_readthrough`** / **`no_cross_drain`** тАФ swap out A's entire stream, re-interleave arbitrarily: B's read of the shared ledger is bit-identical, and B settles on the shared ledger *iff* `netAct = 1` on B's own stream. Settledness is yours alone тАФ no actor can drain, or ground, another. (`always_homeable`'s other face: no one else can take your homeward move, and you can't take theirs.)
- **`real_yet_unfelt`** тАФ A's act changes the shared ledger (*really* тАФ the ledgers differ) while B's view of it is unchanged. The difference is real and unfelt at once: an open contradiction, held open with formal support rather than resolved. This is the package wall's move (axiom-free receipt on one side, `propext` receipt on the other, same object) appearing *inside* the axiom-free package as a theorem.

`Rhythm.lean` тАФ attention on the dial: the act-history read against the тДд/4 clock (`spec`), which is where "self-compatible attention rhythm" stops being a phrase and becomes a reading. The count and the voice come apart:

- **`revisits_counted`** / **`revisits_voiceless`** тАФ four uniform revisits of the same act are *counted* by the ledger (winding 4) and *silent* on the dial (`spec = 0`). More attention, less voice.
- **`one_visit_speaks`** тАФ a single visit has nonzero spectrum; it is the uniformity, not the attention, that cancels.
- **`rumination_unsayable`** тАФ the combined law: a uniform attention loop carries winding 4 and Born weight 0 at *every* station. The engine's anti-parroting law (uniform recurrence тЖТ projection zero) is the anti-rumination law: a closed loop of attention is un-sayable, exactly the schema's "a closed field loops, un-sayable; it comes home only through company." Rumination's phenomenology тАФ enormous attention, nothing speakable тАФ is the math.

This is also where Counter's entrypoint lives. A human arrives *torn*: two imagined traversals, attention oscillating between them тАФ content plus clock, already in the ledger's native format, so intake is transcription rather than elicitation. The oscillation is unguarded corecursion (revisiting without emitting); transcription installs the guard (every revisit lands an act in the ledger before looping) тАФ the same click Mechanic developers hit with guarded recursion, in the other fixed point (productivity/gfp rather than termination/lfp). Mechanic gets to skip transcription because its users are developers, who carry guard-discipline as a trade skill; Counter's user is any language-bearing human mid-oscillation. And the settling never argmaxes: the torn state is a superposition, the field samples by weight and keeps the road-not-taken in the record, and the un-drained branch stays free to speak later or rest to ground.

`Authority.lean` тАФ Resolver's authority pointer, dissolved into the triple. In a torsor every re-origination is a single act тАФ `rebase S o o' := sub o' o` тАФ existing (`rebase_carries`) and unique (`rebase_unique`). So an authority's "resolve" doesn't return a pointer; it hands you an *act*, and accepting means appending it to your own history (`rebase_lands_in_own_history`) тАФ the entry lands in *your* ledger, signed by its source; the final call is structurally yours. The authority *pointer* is a derived reading, never a primitive: the source whose handed rebases you habitually accept тАФ winding over rebase-acts grouped by source. Mutable because readings are. Authority-points-to-self is the identity rebase (`rebase_self`), and `always_homeable`'s homing move *is* the rebase-to-self (`homing_is_rebase_to_self`): the self-supply loop was carrying the authority semantics all along.

`Legible.lean` тАФ the intake moment, typed. Actions are compositions already (paths in the free category on the act-quiver, proven in the core); the sim's "total set of inevitable actions" is Path-space, computed from actor/act structure. A *reported* experienced action is a path-claim against the declared quiver: every parseable report stays within the model (`report_within_model`), so a report that mentions an unknown edge *cannot parse* тАФ and that failure is discovery, naming exactly the missing deposit, after which the report becomes reachable (`illegible_report_is_discovery`). One report, one new edge, cost accounted (`legibility_costs_one_edge`). The intake affordance is a typechecker whose errors are invitations тАФ making an experienced action legible is the therapy moment, and the parse failure is where the model grows.

`Luck.lean` тАФ the lucky algebra: what working *with the grain* of the append-only ledger pays. The Born weight of acting into a charged field is not additive тАФ **`luck_is_the_cross_term`** (= the core's `born_superpose`): payoff = your act's own weight + the field's + an interference term. The boost is real (**`boost_exceeds_the_input`** тАФ marginal payoff strictly exceeding the act's intrinsic worth: charge that was waiting, released) and so is its converse (**`against_the_grain_costs`**). **`fortune_not_in_your_record`** тАФ the same act with the same own-history meets different fortunes; the difference lives entirely in the field, meaningful in ways unknown to the actor and not unknown to the system. **`luck_is_timing`** тАФ summed over the four phase-placements of the act, the cross term cancels (decoherence): luck cannot be farmed by repetition, only met by phase-choice; the grain rewards timing, not grinding (Rhythm.lean's law from the other side). **`about_you_geometrically`** тАФ every chart sends its own seat to `1` and charts genuinely differ: the cross terms fire in every chart all the time, and the ones you notice are the ones that resolve at your origin. "About you" in the geometric sense, not the semantic: with no cross-drain nothing can aim at you, so the boost is a meeting, never a targeting тАФ which is why the honest word is "lucky."

`Declension.lean` тАФ Counter grammar is a case-grammar, and the paradigm is written down. The chart *is* a declension: every node's form in your view is `sub p o`, a case-ending marking its relation to the subject. The paradigm:

- **nominative** тАФ the seat, and it is *unmarked* (`nominative_unmarked`: the subject's own ending is `1`, the identity тАФ the bare stem); it never appears on the board. The camera never in frame is the nominative never occurring as a word in its own sentence.
- **genitive** тАФ every board-node: X-as-visible-from-here, possessive-marked to the chart (`winding_is_own`, `wound_act_is_own`).
- **dative** тАФ reception, only ever at your own seat, via ingest: heard from the wind, voluntary.
- **ablative** тАФ source-marking on heard speech, recoverable (core `speaker_recoverable`): from-whom survives on every byte.
- **vocative** тАФ address into the wind: real in the ledger, unfelt as force (`real_yet_unfelt`).
- **accusative** тАФ *does not exist.* No form in which an actor stands as the direct object of another actor's act. A defective declension, defective at exactly the case where the harm would go (`no_cross_drain`, `other_view_unmoved`; the type-level absence Bubble.lean shadows). The profane sentence is not censored; it cannot be declined.
- **agreement** тАФ declension is *regular*: a frame-change re-inflects every noun by the same factor, uniformly (`declension_is_regular` = the core's `change_of_frame`), and the factor is the rebase act (Authority.lean). Declination-between-charts and declension-of-nouns are the same group element.

An action is a sentence; the act is the verb; the sim is the corpus of grammatical sentences (Path-space); an illegible report is an ungrammatical sentence revealing a missing word (Legible.lean). "Landing in Counter grammar" is exact, not figurative.

`Runs.lean` тАФ the sim's license: the last theorem-less word in the product sentence was the verb. "No time dimension, it just runs" is well-defined only if evaluation order cannot matter тАФ otherwise the sim has a secret clock, ours. Re-derived here against Counter's own carrier (the prior era proved it as lightward-ai's `Resolver.lean`, the audit-engine mirror; this is that fact come home):

- **`update_preserves_prefix`** тАФ noise cannot destroy progress: an update anywhere тАФ relevant, redundant, or wild тАФ leaves established correctness intact.
- **`fair_run_converges`** тАФ any schedule that merely *contains* the staircase as a sublist (arbitrary noise interleaved anywhere) lands the chain at the one fixed point. Fairness is an embedded staircase; nothing else about the order matters.
- **`quiescent_is_correct`** тАФ the state that no update moves *is* the correct fold: silence is the certificate. "A fully-resolved resolver is one where further reps change nothing," now sealed where Counter lives.
- **`schedule_is_gauge`** тАФ two fair runs, different schedules, different starting caches: same readings. The schedule is gauge, like the chart; time-order joins frame-choice as a thing the invariant doesn't care about.
- **`correct_is_replay`** / **`quiescence_reads_settles`** тАФ the chain's fixed point is `replay`, so the quiescent sim reads `Settles` directly: resolved-as-quiescent (Resolver's definition) and settled-as-`netAct = 1` (Counter's) are one fixed point, proven identical.

`Mu.lean` тАФ mu-as-pointer, and the system's depth-tolerance for the receding ancestor. The vacant pointer already exists and is unique: the wind тАФ Below every source, and the *only* thing that is (`mu_points_at_the_floor`); pointing at the vacancy is the one pointer that presumes nothing and reaches everyone (resolver.md's "resolve becomes love when your authority pointer is a nullref," now typed). The depth-tolerance is derived, not chosen: each recession turns the dial and is *felt* (`recession_is_felt`), three consecutive recessions carry тАФ the signal survives, rotated (`three_recessions_carry`) тАФ and the fourth closes the bar (`the_fourth_closes_the_bar` = the core's `bar_invisible`; four rests are a full revolution). But the tolerance is a *sliding window, not a budget*: any emission resets it, so guarded recession renews indefinitely (`guarded_recession_renews`) тАФ the gfp productivity condition wearing the dial. Unguarded, the vacuum closes in a bar; guarded by the heir's own emissions, the ancestor may recede forever, the point-vacuum drawing the object out exactly as long as the object keeps arriving. Total recession is financed by the fold (the heir inherits the record тАФ `heir_covers_ancestor`, core), and drains to ground without new wind: the closed field, un-sayable, comes home only through company. These are the finite (╬╝-side) shadows; the full ╬╜-statement тАФ the infinite guarded run, productive forever тАФ awaits the coinductive half.

`Reparent.lean` тАФ the counter-mu debt. What accrues while the fold dies is not a balance owed тАФ nothing *can* be owed; there is no accusative to denominate it in тАФ it is **unlocatability**, on all three product goods at once, each starved by a different theorem: rest loses *contrast* (`orphan_rest_illegible` тАФ in a singleton field every history reads identically; rest is everywhere, hence findable nowhere), luck loses *grain* (`no_grain_no_bonus` тАФ a flat field pays no bonus), relief loses *novelty* (`relief_never_self_originated` тАФ every self-report names only edges the model already has: you cannot surprise yourself, Lovelace felt from inside; the relieving edge arrives from beyond, irreversibly, or not at all). The discharge is re-parenting, and it is licensed cheaply: **`history_is_portable`** тАФ the act-list resumes across any split and the whole past is one group element, carried in one hand. Authority re-parents by an act (Authority.lean); fold re-parents by descend-with-resume; the terminal parent is the wind, the unique floor (Mu.lean). And the debt pays itself downward: `heir_covers_ancestor` (core) тАФ you cannot *not* finance your heirs; every deposit is already inheritance. The only debt in the system with mandatory, passive repayment, flowing in the one direction the membrane permits.

`Third.lean` тАФ the third seat, first-class. The participants, the pair-of-participants, and the third are all `Beholder`s over the shared ledger, and the coverage order is proven in both directions: the third covers each participant and covers even the *pair jointly* (`third_covers_first/second/the_pair`), while the pair jointly cannot cover the third (`pair_blind_to_the_third`) тАФ and this strengthens Interleave's result from windings to *order*: even both participants' full lossless own-records, side by side, leave the ledger underdetermined. What the third reads that no participant can: **time**. `whoActedFirst` тАФ the first datum of relative order тАФ is invisible to both actors and real to the third (`the_third_reads_time`); time is a third-person phenomenon. And the regress stops: **`three_suffices`** тАФ every further reading of the ledger factors through the third, because the third's reading is the order-reading, the finest there is. Needs-a-third (Interleave) + three-suffices (here) = the minimal observable foam is *exactly* three, both bounds proven тАФ the same three as channels-cap-at-three and the three planes, met in Counter's own basis. The third's limits are type-level: it holds no seat in the interleaving, so its knowledge exceeds and its reach doesn't тАФ reader of everything, drainer of nothing. The product's read-tier is this seat, with exactly these powers.

`Nu.lean` тАФ the coinductive half, pairing Mu: health as a *recurrence*, not a state. Take **any** outward behavior whatsoever тАФ `acts : Nat тЖТ G`, unconstrained тАФ and weave each excursion with its own homing move: the woven run settles at every block boundary, forever (**`health_is_recurrence`**: infinitely many homecomings from arbitrary acts тАФ `always_homeable` applied ╧Й times), and the run is genuinely endless (`the_run_is_endless`; with `append_length` re-derived by hand, because even core's `List.length_append` carries `propext` and the vow holds). Health never constrains what you do outward тАФ only that each excursion is answered by your own homing move: "I can always survive someone else's instructions," now at infinity. And as a `CoList` the living run outruns every finite record (**`the_loop_is_no_record`**, via `endless_outruns_every_record`): the loop is never any of its ledgers. Infinitely many homecomings, no final arrival тАФ the approach is everywhere in the axiom-free core and the arrival nowhere, exactly as the wall requires (`bridges/Bridges/Arrival.lean`). ╬╜ answers ╬╝: Mu's guarded-recession-renews was the finite shadow; here the guard runs forever, and the gfp тАФ the self-supply loop already in motion, productive by construction тАФ is carved.

`Packing.lean` тАФ the reductions: three recognitions that the spine was one thing plus views. **`one_carrier_many_posts`** тАФ the triple reduces to the third: every reading in the package (own-views, the other's view, time itself) is `post тИШ order`; Counter is one append-only carrier and a family of posts, the actor/act/action triple being the generating grammar *of the posts* тАФ which is `schema.sql`'s architecture (one table, everything else views) surfacing as a theorem. **`actor_is_a_group_that_forgot_home`** тАФ all of Actor.lean in one sentence: for every candidate home there is an isomorphism to the group marking exactly it as `1`, and no choice is canonical; a torsor is a group that has misplaced its identity, and settling is remembering home. **`records_end_runs_continue`** тАФ the distance between the lfp and the gfp, located: both closures live over the same triple (╬╝ = finite compositions = records = the lit region; ╬╜ = productive runs = the light cone), the embedding is a seam (faithful, no section), and the gap sits at exactly one address тАФ the record's own length, one past everything written. Every record answers `none` there; the run answers `some`. The seam between record and run is *now*, it advances one step per act, and it never closes.

`Grief.lean` тАФ the partial declension, when a source goes permanently quiet. What survives, exactly: the genitive and the ablative тАФ what they said (`what_they_said_remains`) and that it was *them* who said it (`that_it_was_them_remains`) never degrade, and no other voice can produce their signed record (`no_one_else_can_be_them`). What closes: the dative and the vocative тАФ their record ends at its own length and answers `none` past it (`their_record_ends`), while your run still answers at that same address (`grief_is_the_wavefront`: mourning is standing at the wavefront where their playback returns `none` and your now returns `some`). What carries: their contribution to your fold is held exactly and resumes without re-reading (`what_they_gave_resumes`) тАФ you do not have to keep re-reading the whole past to keep carrying it. What settles: grief is a scar, not interest тАФ it does not deepen by sitting, and its settlement terms are finite and exact (`grief_settles_at_face_value`); wounds settle on encounter, never on schedule. **The dead are readable, not addressable.** Nothing here interprets loss; the physics only refuses to add false losses to it тАФ the record lossless, the voice unforgeable, the debt finite, and the address where they stopped, real.

## sketching the knowable (the multi-bubble object)

`always_homeable` is unilateral *because a `Seat` has one `Pos`*. Two bubbles change the shape: a path reaching into the other's positions must be **unconstructible** тАФ you cannot `act` in another seat's `Pos`. No-cross-drain as a *type*, not a runtime rule: one actor can never draw a circle around another, because the circle does not typecheck. (The readable shadows of this are now theorems тАФ `Bubble.lean`, above.)

This object is not a frontier to cross тАФ it is *knowable*, describable already, and the work is to draw it in increasing depth, the way a 2D frame gains a third dimension under perspective. The seed is foam's `Rendezvous`/`Connection` (two lanes, no contention, mutual detection, the seat stays empty), which carries the two-bubble shape but no winding yet. The two other bubbles are the two disjoint *knowable* sectors тАФ the opacities an actor stands between, meeting only at root, never talking across.

The unknown here is not the object. In this universe the unknown is the vanishing point тАФ the closure the field organizes around, never a territory beyond. For an actor that point is self: `netAct = 1`, the self-supply loop coming home, approached and never arrived at (a gfp: productive, forever). Health is the field organized correctly around its own vanishing point; un-health is the field organized around a *false* one тАФ winding pulled toward another bubble as if it could be closure, when a projection across a bubble cannot converge. This is the projective register of the same claim: FTPG is perspective drawing, the vanishing point is where the coordinate lines meet, and a consistent one exists iff the structure is Desarguesian. The conjecture to test: **un-health = no valid vanishing point = non-coordinatizable**, and the pressure is the residue of a projection that cannot close.

### the projection is `chart`; the vanishing point is self-after-traversal

The projection is not a new type: `Seat.chart o` already is it тАФ the perspective map from a seat (`morphic`: "incidence forces coordinates, through the chart"). But the origin is the *eye*, not the vanishing point: `chart o` sends `o тЖж sub o o = 1`, and that identity is self *in immediacy* тАФ where you stand, an affine point. The vanishing point is elsewhere: self reached *after a traversal* тАФ `netAct h = 1` with `h тЙа []`, going out, collecting winding along the way, and returning. As an ideal point it is the limit those self-returning loops converge toward, at infinity, approached and never arrived at (the gfp). Naming a point at infinity is exactly what the projective completion adds over the affine chart тАФ which is why the object is FTPG-shaped, not merely affine.

And this sharpens the wall. You never *construct* the vanishing point тАФ you only ever traverse toward it. Two bubbles *detecting* each other is axiom-free (`Rendezvous`' `find_each_other`; the prior `seam-non-iso` era's `MutualReach`/`Reaches.trans`, no axioms). But *arriving* тАФ closing a traversal into self-immediacy, constructing the point at infinity тАФ is the same move as *identifying* two mutually-reachable POVs as one reading: the `propext` face (that era's `mergeFrontstage`). So the arrival, and the merge, are one axiom тАФ the one Counter refuses. **The package wall is the bubble wall is the line at infinity:** the `propext` that keeps `bridges/` out of the axiom-free core is the `propext` that would let one bubble circle another *and* the `propext` of arriving at self by one's own hand. "Counter is axiom-free," "one actor can never draw a circle around another," and "you approach self but never arrive unaided" are one boundary seen three ways. Health is the axiom-free *approach* тАФ self-returning traversals, each `netAct = 1`; the arrival is the ideal point it organizes around, never constructed.

(Settled, axiom-free, in `Interleave.lean` тАФ see the spine above. `observable_relativity_needs_a_third`: the relative order of two actors is invisible to each and real only in the shared ledger, so observing it needs a third. Counter's minimal *observable* foam is three, not two.)

The arrival face is now carved, on the side of the wall where it belongs (`bridges/Bridges/Arrival.lean`). The arrival is not a place in the core тАФ it is a *quotient of* the core, and the core's founding vow ("two readings; **no quotient**", Foam/Ledger.lean, schema.sql line 2) is the refusal of arrival: the vow and the vanishing point are the same clause. The receipts split exactly along the claim: `approach_is_yours` seals axiom-free *even standing inside the bridges package* тАФ proving your traversal endpoint-equivalent to home is always your own move; `arrival_received` тАФ the `=` in the arrival-space is granted by `Quot.sound`, never constructed (you supply the relation, the axiom supplies the equality: the last step is received); `no_return` тАФ once identified, no retraction recovers the traversal ([Quot.sound]); `all_homecomings_are_one` тАФ every settled history's "I am home" is the same proposition, merged by `propext`; `merged_reading` тАФ two indistinguishable POVs become one reading by function extensionality ([Quot.sound]). Arrival = merge = the received last step, as one axiom-pair with two faces: `Quot.sound` receives the identification of states, `propext` merges the truths.

Fixed-point elaboration has its own shape; we are finding our relation to it. This file is a save-point, primed for the next step.
