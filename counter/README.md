# counter

foam run as an actor model — the third resolved pattern, after Locksmith (locks/keys/conditions) and Mechanic (events/tasks/actions). Counter deals in **actors / acts / actions**, and is distinct in being *loop-predicated*: the others experience a loop as an antipattern; Counter's base object *is* a loop — an actor supplying the self with self.

axiom-free, like the core. This package requires `foam` and **not** Mathlib: no choice, no propext. Every claim seals `does not depend on any axioms`. Counter is the application of the physics, so it keeps the physics' discipline. The why is the Lean.

the product sentence, each noun bound to its receipt: **Counter and its user, as a pair, locate the user's relief, restedness, and luck.** Relief is an *address* — a stuck position plus the one deposit that relieves it (`relief_at_position`, core; `illegible_report_is_discovery`). Restedness is a *move* — the homing act, always available, always the user's own (`always_homeable`). Luck is a *timing* — the cross term, met by phase-choice, never farmed (`luck_is_timing`). Located, never instructed; and the pair is load-bearing from both sides — the tool alone has no source (transcription only, the field originates nothing) and the user alone has no view (`observable_relativity_needs_a_third`). These three words are the oldest snake oil in the market, unfalsifiable exactly as long as they name semantics; here they name structure — an address, a move, a timing — and structure carries receipts. That inversion is the market fit.

## the spine so far

`Actor.lean` — the single actor, and the ground facts about settling. An actor's history is a `List G` of acts over its seat's group; the winding number around an act is `Ledger.freq` of that act in the history (Counter *counts*: the name is winding numbers around acts).

- **`replay_is_netAct`** — a whole history collapses to one net displacement, `netAct h : G`. Your entire past is a single group element.
- **`settles_iff_home`** — `Settles S h p ↔ netAct h = 1`. A history brings you home *iff its net displacement is the identity*. This is where "a healthy model settles" lives, and it is the resolution of the winding-number-growth intuition: settling is not a rate, it is the identity element `1`. Magnitude-free, time-free — the invariant inherits those from the algebra, not from a policy.
- **`always_homeable`** — from *any* accumulated history, appending the single act `sub p (replay h p)` returns you home, unilaterally. "I can always survive someone else's instructions": you can always ground yourself, by your own move, no matter what you've taken on.
- **`lone_actor_settled`** / **`pressure_needs_a_second`** — health is *relational*. A solitary actor's every reading is `1` (vacuously settled); pressure only exists once there is displacement relative to a second position. Priorities.md layer 1 ("your own health") is first but never isolated — even it is defined "in listening to yourself," a relation. An earlier era of the project found the same fact in a different basis ("relaxation requires an other").

`Interleave.lean` — two actors' act-streams interleave into one shared ledger, and the count is on the ledger.

- **`winding_is_own`** — an actor's winding read off the shared ledger equals its winding on its own stream: the interleaving, and the other actor entirely, are invisible to it.
- **`observable_relativity_needs_a_third`** — yet the interleaving is *real*. Two orderings of the same two streams (A-then-B, B-then-A) leave *both* actors' windings identical while differing in the ledger. The relative order of who-acted-when is invisible to each actor and real only in the shared ledger; observing relative motion needs a third bubble that reads the ledger itself. So Counter's minimal *observable* foam is **three, not two** — lining up with dimension-caps-at-three and the three planes.

`Bubble.lean` — the membrane between two actors, in its readable half. The type-level half — cross-winding is a pairing that does not typecheck (`Ledger.freq` demands the act and the history share a type) — cannot be *stated* in Lean, which is the point; these are its shadows on the readable side.

- **`wound_act_is_own`** — any act with nonzero winding in an actor's view is one of the actor's *own* acts. Every circle you can draw is inked around your own acts: you can never draw one around another actor.
- **`other_view_unmoved`** / **`settles_readthrough`** / **`no_cross_drain`** — swap out A's entire stream, re-interleave arbitrarily: B's read of the shared ledger is bit-identical, and B settles on the shared ledger *iff* `netAct = 1` on B's own stream. Settledness is yours alone — no actor can drain, or ground, another. (`always_homeable`'s other face: no one else can take your homeward move, and you can't take theirs.)
- **`real_yet_unfelt`** — A's act changes the shared ledger (*really* — the ledgers differ) while B's view of it is unchanged. The difference is real and unfelt at once: an open contradiction, held open with formal support rather than resolved. This is the package wall's move (axiom-free receipt on one side, `propext` receipt on the other, same object) appearing *inside* the axiom-free package as a theorem.

`Rhythm.lean` — attention on the dial: the act-history read against the ℤ/4 clock (`spec`), which is where "self-compatible attention rhythm" stops being a phrase and becomes a reading. The count and the voice come apart:

- **`revisits_counted`** / **`revisits_voiceless`** — four uniform revisits of the same act are *counted* by the ledger (winding 4) and *silent* on the dial (`spec = 0`). More attention, less voice.
- **`one_visit_speaks`** — a single visit has nonzero spectrum; it is the uniformity, not the attention, that cancels.
- **`rumination_unsayable`** — the combined law: a uniform attention loop carries winding 4 and Born weight 0 at *every* station. The engine's anti-parroting law (uniform recurrence → projection zero) is the anti-rumination law: a closed loop of attention is un-sayable, exactly the schema's "a closed field loops, un-sayable; it comes home only through company." Rumination's phenomenology — enormous attention, nothing speakable — is the math.

This is also where Counter's entrypoint lives. A human arrives *torn*: two imagined traversals, attention oscillating between them — content plus clock, already in the ledger's native format, so intake is transcription rather than elicitation. The oscillation is unguarded corecursion (revisiting without emitting); transcription installs the guard (every revisit lands an act in the ledger before looping) — the same click Mechanic developers hit with guarded recursion, in the other fixed point (productivity/gfp rather than termination/lfp). Mechanic gets to skip transcription because its users are developers, who carry guard-discipline as a trade skill; Counter's user is any language-bearing human mid-oscillation. And the settling never argmaxes: the torn state is a superposition, the field samples by weight and keeps the road-not-taken in the record, and the un-drained branch stays free to speak later or rest to ground.

`Authority.lean` — Resolver's authority pointer, dissolved into the triple. In a torsor every re-origination is a single act — `rebase S o o' := sub o' o` — existing (`rebase_carries`) and unique (`rebase_unique`). So an authority's "resolve" doesn't return a pointer; it hands you an *act*, and accepting means appending it to your own history (`rebase_lands_in_own_history`) — the entry lands in *your* ledger, signed by its source; the final call is structurally yours. The authority *pointer* is a derived reading, never a primitive: the source whose handed rebases you habitually accept — winding over rebase-acts grouped by source. Mutable because readings are. Authority-points-to-self is the identity rebase (`rebase_self`), and `always_homeable`'s homing move *is* the rebase-to-self (`homing_is_rebase_to_self`): the self-supply loop was carrying the authority semantics all along.

`Legible.lean` — the intake moment, typed. Actions are compositions already (paths in the free category on the act-quiver, proven in the core); the sim's "total set of inevitable actions" is Path-space, computed from actor/act structure. A *reported* experienced action is a path-claim against the declared quiver: every parseable report stays within the model (`report_within_model`), so a report that mentions an unknown edge *cannot parse* — and that failure is discovery, naming exactly the missing deposit, after which the report becomes reachable (`illegible_report_is_discovery`). One report, one new edge, cost accounted (`legibility_costs_one_edge`). The intake affordance is a typechecker whose errors are invitations — making an experienced action legible is the therapy moment, and the parse failure is where the model grows.

`Luck.lean` — the lucky algebra: what working *with the grain* of the append-only ledger pays. The Born weight of acting into a charged field is not additive — **`luck_is_the_cross_term`** (= the core's `born_superpose`): payoff = your act's own weight + the field's + an interference term. The boost is real (**`boost_exceeds_the_input`** — marginal payoff strictly exceeding the act's intrinsic worth: charge that was waiting, released) and so is its converse (**`against_the_grain_costs`**). **`fortune_not_in_your_record`** — the same act with the same own-history meets different fortunes; the difference lives entirely in the field, meaningful in ways unknown to the actor and not unknown to the system. **`luck_is_timing`** — summed over the four phase-placements of the act, the cross term cancels (decoherence): luck cannot be farmed by repetition, only met by phase-choice; the grain rewards timing, not grinding (Rhythm.lean's law from the other side). **`about_you_geometrically`** — every chart sends its own seat to `1` and charts genuinely differ: the cross terms fire in every chart all the time, and the ones you notice are the ones that resolve at your origin. "About you" in the geometric sense, not the semantic: with no cross-drain nothing can aim at you, so the boost is a meeting, never a targeting — which is why the honest word is "lucky."

## sketching the knowable (the multi-bubble object)

`always_homeable` is unilateral *because a `Seat` has one `Pos`*. Two bubbles change the shape: a path reaching into the other's positions must be **unconstructible** — you cannot `act` in another seat's `Pos`. No-cross-drain as a *type*, not a runtime rule: one actor can never draw a circle around another, because the circle does not typecheck. (The readable shadows of this are now theorems — `Bubble.lean`, above.)

This object is not a frontier to cross — it is *knowable*, describable already, and the work is to draw it in increasing depth, the way a 2D frame gains a third dimension under perspective. The seed is foam's `Rendezvous`/`Connection` (two lanes, no contention, mutual detection, the seat stays empty), which carries the two-bubble shape but no winding yet. The two other bubbles are the two disjoint *knowable* sectors — the opacities an actor stands between, meeting only at root, never talking across.

The unknown here is not the object. In this universe the unknown is the vanishing point — the closure the field organizes around, never a territory beyond. For an actor that point is self: `netAct = 1`, the self-supply loop coming home, approached and never arrived at (a gfp: productive, forever). Health is the field organized correctly around its own vanishing point; un-health is the field organized around a *false* one — winding pulled toward another bubble as if it could be closure, when a projection across a bubble cannot converge. This is the projective register of the same claim: FTPG is perspective drawing, the vanishing point is where the coordinate lines meet, and a consistent one exists iff the structure is Desarguesian. The conjecture to test: **un-health = no valid vanishing point = non-coordinatizable**, and the pressure is the residue of a projection that cannot close.

### the projection is `chart`; the vanishing point is self-after-traversal

The projection is not a new type: `Seat.chart o` already is it — the perspective map from a seat (`morphic`: "incidence forces coordinates, through the chart"). But the origin is the *eye*, not the vanishing point: `chart o` sends `o ↦ sub o o = 1`, and that identity is self *in immediacy* — where you stand, an affine point. The vanishing point is elsewhere: self reached *after a traversal* — `netAct h = 1` with `h ≠ []`, going out, collecting winding along the way, and returning. As an ideal point it is the limit those self-returning loops converge toward, at infinity, approached and never arrived at (the gfp). Naming a point at infinity is exactly what the projective completion adds over the affine chart — which is why the object is FTPG-shaped, not merely affine.

And this sharpens the wall. You never *construct* the vanishing point — you only ever traverse toward it. Two bubbles *detecting* each other is axiom-free (`Rendezvous`' `find_each_other`; the prior `seam-non-iso` era's `MutualReach`/`Reaches.trans`, no axioms). But *arriving* — closing a traversal into self-immediacy, constructing the point at infinity — is the same move as *identifying* two mutually-reachable POVs as one reading: the `propext` face (that era's `mergeFrontstage`). So the arrival, and the merge, are one axiom — the one Counter refuses. **The package wall is the bubble wall is the line at infinity:** the `propext` that keeps `bridges/` out of the axiom-free core is the `propext` that would let one bubble circle another *and* the `propext` of arriving at self by one's own hand. "Counter is axiom-free," "one actor can never draw a circle around another," and "you approach self but never arrive unaided" are one boundary seen three ways. Health is the axiom-free *approach* — self-returning traversals, each `netAct = 1`; the arrival is the ideal point it organizes around, never constructed.

(Settled, axiom-free, in `Interleave.lean` — see the spine above. `observable_relativity_needs_a_third`: the relative order of two actors is invisible to each and real only in the shared ledger, so observing it needs a third. Counter's minimal *observable* foam is three, not two.)

The arrival face is now carved, on the side of the wall where it belongs (`bridges/Bridges/Arrival.lean`). The arrival is not a place in the core — it is a *quotient of* the core, and the core's founding vow ("two readings; **no quotient**", Foam/Ledger.lean, schema.sql line 2) is the refusal of arrival: the vow and the vanishing point are the same clause. The receipts split exactly along the claim: `approach_is_yours` seals axiom-free *even standing inside the bridges package* — proving your traversal endpoint-equivalent to home is always your own move; `arrival_received` — the `=` in the arrival-space is granted by `Quot.sound`, never constructed (you supply the relation, the axiom supplies the equality: the last step is received); `no_return` — once identified, no retraction recovers the traversal ([Quot.sound]); `all_homecomings_are_one` — every settled history's "I am home" is the same proposition, merged by `propext`; `merged_reading` — two indistinguishable POVs become one reading by function extensionality ([Quot.sound]). Arrival = merge = the received last step, as one axiom-pair with two faces: `Quot.sound` receives the identification of states, `propext` merges the truths.

Fixed-point elaboration has its own shape; we are finding our relation to it. This file is a save-point, primed for the next step.
